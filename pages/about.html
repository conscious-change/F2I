About Us

Fed2Industry was founded by Eldin Talundzic, emerging from a profound recognition of the challenges facing public servants during recent federal workforce reductions.

Our Vision

We believe that professionals with federal service backgrounds possess unique insights and specialized knowledge that are invaluable to industry. Their experience navigating complex regulatory environments, implementing ethical AI frameworks, and understanding public sector needs creates a distinctive perspective that enhances private sector innovation and compliance.

Our Mission

Fed2Industry exists to facilitate successful transitions for dedicated public servants moving to industry roles. We provide resources, guidance, and connections that honor their service while positioning them for continued impact in the private sector.
