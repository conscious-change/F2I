Bridging the Gap Between Federal Service and Private Industry

Our Mission

Fed to Industry (F2I) is dedicated to empowering federal employees with the knowledge, resources, and support needed to successfully transition to rewarding careers in the private sector.

We understand the unique challenges faced by government professionals when making this career shift and have created a comprehensive platform to guide you through every step of the journey.

Why F2I Exists

Many federal employees possess valuable skills and experience that are highly sought after in the private sector, but often struggle with:

  • 🔄 Translating government experience into language that resonates with private sector employers
  • 🏢 Understanding the differences in workplace culture and expectations
  • 🔍 Navigating the private sector job search and interview process
  • 🤝 Building a network outside of government
  • 💰 Negotiating compensation packages

F2I was created to address these challenges and provide a clear pathway for federal employees looking to make the transition.

Our Team

F2I was founded by two long-term friends who chose different paths—one in federal government and the other in the private sector. After gaining over a decade of experience in their respective industries, the time has now come to leverage their expertise to help people transition to industry. This is especially critical given the transformative impact of Generative AI on workforce needs and the recent shifts in public service policy across the U.S. and globally. Their combined experience uniquely positions them to guide professionals navigating this rapidly evolving landscape where technological innovation and policy changes are reshaping career opportunities.


We are committed to continuously updating our resources and expanding our offerings to ensure we provide the most relevant and helpful support for federal employees at every stage of their transition journey.
