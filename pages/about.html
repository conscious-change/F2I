Our Mission

Fed to Industry (F2I) is dedicated to empowering federal employees with the knowledge, resources, and support needed to successfully transition to rewarding careers in the private sector. We understand the unique challenges faced by government professionals when making this career shift and have created a comprehensive platform to guide you through every step of the journey.

Why F2I Exists

Many federal employees possess valuable skills and experience that are highly sought after in the private sector, but often struggle with:

  • Translating government experience into language that resonates with private sector employers
  • Understanding the differences in workplace culture and expectations
  • Navigating the private sector job search and interview process
  • Building a network outside of government
  • Negotiating compensation packages

F2I was created to address these challenges and provide a clear pathway for federal employees looking to make the transition.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Resources

Curated guides, articles, and tools specifically designed for federal employees transitioning to the private sector.

Skill Translation

Tools to help you translate your government experience into terms that resonate with private sector employers.

Resume & Personal Branding

Guidance on crafting resumes and building a personal brand that highlights your transferable skills.

Interview Preparation

Resources to help you prepare for private sector interviews, including common questions and effective responses.

Networking Strategies

Advice on building a professional network outside of government to support your career transition.

Community Support

Connect with others who have successfully made the transition from federal service to the private sector.

Our Team

F2I was founded by former federal employees who successfully transitioned to the private sector. Our team combines decades of experience in both government and industry, giving us unique insight into the challenges and opportunities of making this career change.

We are committed to continuously updating our resources and expanding our offerings to ensure we provide the most relevant and helpful support for federal employees at every stage of their transition journey.
