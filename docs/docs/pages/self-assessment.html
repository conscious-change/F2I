This self-assessment will help you identify transferable skills and achievements from your federal career that are valuable in the private sector. Complete each section at your own pace. Save & Continue Later

Your responses are automatically saved in your browser as you work. If you need to continue on a different device or browser, you can save your progress to a file and load it later:

  1. Save your progress - Click the "Save to File" button to download your responses as a JSON file
  2. Continue later - When you return, click "Load from File" to upload your saved responses and continue where you left off
