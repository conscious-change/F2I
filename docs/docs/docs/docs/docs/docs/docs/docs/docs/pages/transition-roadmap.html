Transition Roadmap

Your step-by-step guide to successfully transitioning from federal service to the private sector

Your Path to Success in the Private Sector

Click the print button to print the roadmap or explore the detailed steps below.

Transitioning from federal service to the private sector can seem daunting, but with the right approach, it can be a smooth and rewarding process. This roadmap breaks down the journey into manageable steps to help you navigate your career transition successfully.

Pro Tip: We recommend bookmarking this page or pinning it in your browser so you can easily return to it as you progress through your transition journey.

1

Self-Assessment & Goal Setting

Begin by evaluating your skills, interests, values, and career aspirations.

  • Identify your transferable skills and strengths
  • Clarify your career goals and target industries
  • Assess your work preferences and values
  • Determine your salary expectations and lifestyle needs

2

Research & Industry Exploration

Gain a deep understanding of your target industries and potential roles.

  • Research industries that align with your skills and interests
  • Identify roles where your federal experience adds value
  • Understand private sector culture and expectations
  • Research compensation ranges and benefits
Industry Research Guide ‚Üí
3

Skill Translation & Development

Bridge any skill gaps and learn to communicate your experience effectively.

  • Translate your government experience into private sector terminology
  • Identify and address skill gaps through training or education
  • Develop industry-specific knowledge
  • Obtain relevant certifications if necessary
Skill Translation Guide ‚Üí
4

Resume & Personal Branding

Create a compelling private sector resume and establish your professional brand.

  • Develop a results-oriented resume highlighting transferable skills
  • Create or update your LinkedIn profile
  • Build your personal brand online
  • Prepare your elevator pitch
Resume & Branding Guide ‚Üí
5

Networking & Relationship Building

Expand your professional network beyond government circles.

  • Connect with industry professionals and former federal employees
  • Attend industry events, conferences, and meetups
  • Conduct informational interviews
  • Join professional associations in your target industry
Networking Strategy Guide ‚Üí
6

Job Search Strategy

Develop a targeted approach to finding opportunities in the private sector.

  • Create a job search plan with specific targets and timelines
  • Identify companies that value federal experience
  • Leverage job boards and company career pages
  • Work with recruiters who specialize in government transitions
Job Search Strategy Guide ‚Üí
7

Interview Preparation

Prepare to excel in private sector interviews.

  • Practice answering behavioral and technical interview questions
  • Prepare stories that demonstrate your achievements and skills
  • Research companies thoroughly before interviews
  • Prepare questions to ask potential employers
Interview Preparation Guide ‚Üí
8

Compensation Negotiation

Learn to negotiate competitive compensation packages.

  • Research industry salary ranges and total compensation
  • Understand the differences between federal and private sector benefits
  • Develop negotiation strategies
  • Consider the total value proposition beyond salary
Compensation Guide ‚Üí
9

Transition & Onboarding

Successfully navigate your exit from federal service and entry into the private sector.

  • Understand federal resignation procedures and benefits implications
  • Prepare for cultural differences in the private sector
  • Develop strategies for a successful first 90 days
  • Build relationships in your new organization
