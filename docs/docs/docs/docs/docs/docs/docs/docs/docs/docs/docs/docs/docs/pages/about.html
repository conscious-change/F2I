About F2I

Helping federal employees successfully transition to rewarding careers in the private sector

Our Mission

Fed 2 Industry (F2I) is dedicated to empowering federal employees with the knowledge, resources, and support needed to successfully transition to rewarding careers in the private sector.

We understand the unique challenges faced by government professionals when making this career shift and have created a comprehensive platform to guide you through every step of the journey.

The F2I Story

This initiative stemmed from a personal conversation with my wife, where we discussed the challenges facing public servants amid recent mass layoffs in the public sector. We decided we needed to do something to help people who have dedicated their lives to public service transition successfully to industry.

To meet today's complex challenges, industry must be equipped with professionals who bring federal service backgrounds and their unique perspectives. These individuals offer invaluable insights into critical growth areas including the ethical implementation of GenAI in the public sector, AI governance frameworks, regulatory considerations, and much more.
