Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Fed2Industry, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you maintain complete control over your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we handle any data you provide while using our website and tools.

No Personal Identifiable Information

We never ask for personal identifiable information. Our platform is designed to help you with your career transition without requiring any personal details such as:

  • Names or usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Social security numbers
  • Home or work addresses
  • Date of birth or age
  • Any other information that could directly identify you

This approach ensures your privacy is protected from the start.

Local Data Storage

Your data stays on your device. All information you enter into our self-assessment tools, career planning resources, and other interactive features is stored locally on your computer using your browser's local storage capabilities. This means:

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers
  • Your data remains on your device and is only accessible to you
  • Your information persists between sessions on the same device and browser
  • Clearing your browser cache or local storage will remove this data

No Tracking or Advertising

We do not:

  • Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Collect personal information for marketing
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your information for targeted advertising
  • Track your browsing behavior across other websites

Voluntary Data Sharing

You may choose to voluntarily share your skills profile data with us to help improve our services. If you decide to do this:

  • You can export your skills assessment as a JSON file and email it to findjob@fed2industry.com
  • We will remove any personally identifiable information before analyzing the data
  • Your shared data will be used anonymously to identify skill trends and improve our career recommendations
  • This information may help us share appropriate job postings with the community, plan relevant conference topics, and facilitate professional connections
  • Sharing your data is completely optional and not required to use our services

Data Security

While your data is stored locally on your device, we recommend taking these precautions:

  • Use updated browsers with strong security features
  • Be cautious when using public or shared computers
  • Consider exporting and saving your data if you need to access it across multiple devices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post any changes on this page and encourage you to review our privacy policy regularly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at contact@fed2industry.com.
